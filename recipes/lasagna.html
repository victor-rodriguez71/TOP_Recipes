Lasagna

Cheesy lasanga: perfect after a long day

This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.

BONUS: it’s make-ahead and freezer-friendly!

Ingredients

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese, 2 cups
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • Ground Beef (Seasoned how you like)
  • Meat sauce
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Combine all cheeses
  3. Add noodles to water
  4. Brown and seasoon ground beef
  5. Layer cooked noodles, meat, sause, and cheese until pan is full
